# Privacy Policy

## About This Project

This website is an **educational demonstration project** created for the Panaversity Hackathon. It is designed to teach Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics concepts.

## Data Collection

This is a **demo/educational project** with minimal data handling:

### What We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal identification information
- We do not track users across websites
- We do not sell or share user data
- We do not use advertising trackers

### What May Be Temporarily Processed
- **Chat Messages:** When using the AI Study Assistant, your questions are sent to our backend to generate responses. These are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored for tracking purposes.
- **Language Preference:** Your selected language (English, Japanese, Urdu) is stored locally in your browser.
